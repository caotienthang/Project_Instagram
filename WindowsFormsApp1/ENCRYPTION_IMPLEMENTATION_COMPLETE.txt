## 📋 Final Payload Format:

```
Offset | Size | Field           | Value/Description
-------|------|-----------------|----------------------------------
0      | 1    | version         | 0x01 (fixed)
1      | 1    | key_id          | 0xE3 (227 decimal)
2      | 12   | IV              | Random nonce for GCM
14     | 2    | length          | Length of encrypted_key (0x0100 = 256)
16     | 256  | encrypted_key   | RSA(AES_KEY, PKCS1)
272    | 16   | tag             | GCM authentication tag
288    | n    | ciphertext      | AES-GCM(password)

Total: 288 + len(password) bytes
```
